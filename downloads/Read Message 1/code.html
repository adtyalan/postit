My dearest friend,

It has been too long since I last heard the rustle of paper between my fingers. The digital world moves so fast, blurring days into a continuous scroll of glass and light. But here, in the texture of this page, time slows down.

I hope this letter finds you well. I sealed it with the hope that the physical act of opening it brings you a momentary pause—a tiny, tactile joy in your day.

Yours truly,

A Stranger
